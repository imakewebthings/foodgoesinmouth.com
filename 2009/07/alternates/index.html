  • Same Mini Kaiser Rolls

I didn’t like any part of how the arugula was just slapped on. Holy crap at the difference some lemon zest and garlic can make. A couple nights later I went down a different road.

Barbecued Chicken Breast Stuffed w/ Pear, Arugula, & Gorgonzola

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast, boneless and skinless
  • 1/8″ slices of fresh pear
  • Gorgonzola, small crumbles
  • Baby Arugula
  • Lime, for juicing
  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  1. Take a paring or utility knife and cut a small slit in the side of the chicken breast.
  2. While trying to minimize the size of the cut on the outside, work the knife inside the breast to create a hollow pouch.
  3. Stuff the chicken breast pouch thingy with two slices of pear, a tablespoon of Gorgonzola, and as much arugula after the fact as it can handle.
  4. Sew up the hole in the chicken breast up by using one or more toothpicks. Salt liberally.
  5. Throw it on a hot charcoal barbecue and cook evenly until you get an internal temperature reading on a thermometer of 165&deg F. Every couple minutes squeeze some lime juice on it.
  6. Move to a plate. Remove the toothpicks. Let rest for 10 minutes.

That’s it. The whole thing just made me shrug. It was tasty enough, but seemed a bit boring. So back to the sliders I went with some changes.
